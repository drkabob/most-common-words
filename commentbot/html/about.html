About the Project

The "Most Common Words" was started in 2013 by programmer Nathan Hakak, inspired by his friend Johnny. Writing up a quick script and posting it on his website (as well as gitHub), Nathan sat back as he watched his work grow. His best friend Samuel volunteered to design the site's UI (since Nathan was using some basic HTML at the time), and thus the beautiful layout you see before you was born.
